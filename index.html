Once upon a time, in the African kingdom of Zandor, two sweethearts King Kofi and Queen Adwoa got married and planned on having one son, someday ruling the kingdom but on the day of birth, twin boys named Olu and Uba were born. The two brothers were best friends and inseparable until one day the kingdom's ruling was in jeopardy and their father, king Kofi, was near death. It was then that he declared that the twin princes fight for the right to rule the kingdom, for only one of them could be king.
