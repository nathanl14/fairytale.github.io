Once upon a time, in the African kingdom of Zandor, two sweethearts King Kofi and Queen Adwoa got married and planned on having one son, someday ruling the kingdom but on the day of birth, twin boys named Olu and Uba were born. The two brothers were best friends and inseparable until one day the kingdom's ruling was in jeopardy and their father, king Kofi, was near death. It was then that he declared that the twin princes fight for the right to rule the kingdom, for only one of them could be king.

Page 2

The people were shocked but they did not complain, as long as long as the got a new king. At noon everyone gathered in the Kingdom Courtyard and there was lots of speculation about who would win this battle for the throne since they were twin brothers. The fight began as they both bowed to each other and the umpire yells "fight".

Page 3

They both fought with everything they had, as they threw punches and kicks. But finally after many hours and much blood loss, the umpire announced that Olu was the winner and he would be become the new king over his brother, Uba.

Page 4

Uba became jealous and this feeling of jealousy turned into resentment and eventually, Olu became so consumed by his own desire for power he began using dark magic to torment people and become more powerful. King Kofi eventually died and Olu succeded his father. After Olu heard about Uba wrongdoings, as king he had no choice but to banish his own brother from the kingdom of Zandor.
